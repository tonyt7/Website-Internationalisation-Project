Recommended Food Places

Indian Coffee House

Indian Coffee House is one of the oldest restaurant chains in Kerala. And, if you live in or visit Trivandrum, it is a must-visit place. The filter coffee served here is refreshing. You can also order some dosa and idli here.

Speciality:

Filter coffee, Idili and Dosa

Central Road, Trivandrum >

Grand Pavilion

As we talk about Kerala food paradises, we cannot just miss out on mentioning Grand Pavilion. It is one of the oldest food eateries in Kochi where you can order some of the most delectable dishes like Karimeen Pollichathu, Biriyani, Seafood, etc. You will also get various Syrian food options here.

Speciality:

Karimeen Pollichathu, Biriyani & Seafood

Ernakulam,Kochi >

Kethel's Chicken

If you have heard about Kerala’s fried chicken, you must visit Kethal’s Chicken. They have been serving since 1949 and is one of the most sought after restaurants now in Trivandrum. Try chicken fry with chapatti here.

Speciality:

Chicken fry with chapati

Rahamaniya Lane, Trivandrum >

Shala Restaurant

If you miss home-cooked food, you can head to Shala Restaurant without a second thought. The chefs of this restaurant are the local housewives, who cook authentic home-style Kerala food. Some of the special dishes here are kingfish steak and Malabar prawn curry.

Speciality:

Kingfish steak and Malabar prawn curry

Peter Celli Street, Kochi >

Rahmathullah Hotel

If you are looking for the best biryani in Kerala, you must visit Rahmathullah Hotel. Operating since 1948, Rahmathullah Hotel is a favorite restaurant of many in Kochi, especially the biryani lovers. They offer a wide variety of biryani; however, some of them are served on specific days of the week.

Speciality:

Fish Biriyani and Prawn Biriyani

Aanavaadal,Kochi >

Rapsy Restaurant

We all try to avoid eating out at the end of the month, but if you feel like munching some good food during those days, you can visit Rapsy Restaurant. Dishes like biryani and Rapsy’s special paratha start at just Rs 50. Are you already making your plan to visit Rapsy now?

Speciality:

Biriyani and Rapsy's special paratha

Main Bazaar,Munnar >

Hotel Annapoorna

Kerala is famous for dishes like prawn curry, seafood chemmeen curry, etc. However, if you are a vegetarian, you do not have to feel bad, as there are multiple vegetarian options as well. From puttu to masala dosa, and even idli, you can order any vegetarian dish here, and you will get it.

Speciality:

Prawn curry & Seafood

Perumbavoor,Kerala >
